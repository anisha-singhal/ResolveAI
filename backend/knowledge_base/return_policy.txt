Our standard return policy allows for returns of any unworn, unwashed merchandise within 30 days of the original purchase date. To be eligible for a full refund, the item must be in its original packaging with all tags attached. The customer is responsible for return shipping costs. To initiate a return, please visit our online portal and enter your order number and email address. Refunds are processed to the original form of payment within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. Final sale items are not eligible for return or exchange.
