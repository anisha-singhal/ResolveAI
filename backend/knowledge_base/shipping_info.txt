Standard shipping within the continental US takes 5-7 business days. Expedited shipping takes 2-3 business days. International shipping times vary by destination but typically take 10-15 business days. We primarily use FedEx and USPS for our deliveries. Once an order is shipped, a tracking number will be sent to the a customer's registered email address. Shipping is free on all orders over $50. For orders under $50, a flat rate of $5.99 applies.
